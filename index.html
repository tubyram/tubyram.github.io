Window Treatment Solutions Across DFW

What We've Learned Working in These Communities:

Southlake families frequently ask about child safety features for their roller shades - especially with so many young kids in the neighborhood. We always recommend cordless options or motorized controls to keep little ones safe.

Grapevine's historic homes are especially fun to work with for shutter installation. Those beautiful older windows often have unique architectural details and character that you just don't find in newer construction.

We're seeing more professionals in the area choosing electric and smart roller shades for the convenience factor. Nothing beats adjusting your window treatments from your phone during a video call or having them automatically close at sunset.

For new construction projects, we've found that involving us early in the building process makes a huge difference. Builders appreciate having custom window covering plans sorted out before drywall goes up - it saves everyone time and headaches later.
