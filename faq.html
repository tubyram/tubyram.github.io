How do I choose the right window treatment for my space?

We help you narrow it down during your free consultation based on lighting, privacy, aesthetics, and your lifestyle. For example, roller shades are sleek and modern, while plantation shutters offer timeless elegance and light control.

Do you install the products you sell?

Yes! We provide full-service installation to ensure a perfect fit and finish. Our installers are experienced and respectful of your home—we treat it like our own.

How long does the installation process take?

Installation times vary based on the size and complexity of the project, but most installations can be completed in a few hours. We’ll provide a more accurate estimate during your consultation.

What is your warranty policy?

We offer a limited lifetime warranty on all our products, covering defects in materials and workmanship. We stand behind our work and are here to help if you have any issues.

Do you offer motorized or automated window treatments?

Yes! We offer a range of motorized window treatments—including roller shades, cellular shades, and even plantation shutters—that can be controlled via remote, smartphone app, or home automation system. They’re quiet, precise, and perfect for hard-to-reach windows or luxury convenience.

Can I control my window treatments with my phone or smart home system?

Absolutely. Our motorized systems are compatible with apps, timers, and smart home platforms like Alexa, Google Home, and Apple HomeKit. You can schedule shades to open with the sunrise, close at sunset, or respond to a tap on your phone—wherever you are.

Is it possible to automate blinds and shades based on time of day or sunlight?

Yes! Many of our motorized window treatments allow for advanced automation, including schedules based on time of day, sunlight exposure, or room temperature. It's a great way to improve energy efficiency and comfort without lifting a finger.
